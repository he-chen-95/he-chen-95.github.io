About me

Hi, I am Chen HE，you can call me Chen. I am a Junior Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer and currently studying in France.

I've been programming since I was a graduate student, writing web pages and mobile applications, using machine learning and data mining algorithms to discover knowledge and patterns from some publicly available datasets.

After graduating from XIDIAN University (Xi'an, China), I received three years of basic training in Computer Science at a French Grande École in engineering: Télécom Saint-Etienne (Saint-Etienne, France). From 2018, I started to specialize in the Machine Learning domain; and I got my double master's degree in Data Science from Jean Monnet University (Saint-Etienne, France) in 2019. I got accepted for a Ph.D. in industrial engineering at École des Mines de Saint-Étienne (Saint-Etienne, France) since Oct 2019. I am currently working on the prediction of hospital code using Electronic Health Records (EHRs) for University Hospital of Saint Etienne.

Research area

I'm interested in Machine Learning and Data Science. Much of my research is about Data Analytics and Natural Language Processing. Please contact me through E-mail or LinkedIn 👉 LinkedIn·Chen if you have any comments or questions.

Interest
